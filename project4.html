The Rules of The Game Movie Poster

Tool(s): Adobe Illustrator

Project Description: I created a poster based on the short story The Rules of The Game by Amy Tan. I used Adobe Illustrtaor to create a circular pattern resembling a chess board. I then created the pawn and crown and placed in the center to emphasize a pawn winning the game.
